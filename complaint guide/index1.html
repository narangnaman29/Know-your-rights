How to file a complaint

Police Complaint

A police complaint, often referred to as an FIR (First Information Report), is a formal written document filed with law enforcement to report a crime or seek their intervention in a legal matter. It includes details about the incident, location, date, parties involved, and alleged offense, serving as the initial step in the criminal justice process.

Consumer Complaint

Consumer complaints are objections by individuals or customers about unsatisfactory products, services, or experiences with businesses. They cover issues like product defects, service quality, fraud, and unfair practices, offering consumers a means to seek resolution and protection in commercial transactions.

Education Complaint

Education complaints address concerns within the educational system and can originate from students, parents, educators, or stakeholders. They encompass academic matters, administrative issues, and grievances related to curriculum, teaching standards, admissions, fees, and facilities.

Healthcare Complaint

Healthcare complaints express dissatisfaction or concern about the quality of medical services or care received from healthcare providers. These complaints include issues like misdiagnosis, medical errors, inadequate treatment, and billing disputes, aiming for resolution, accountability, and healthcare system improvements.

Transportation Complaint

Transportation complaints involve grievances with various transportation services, including public and private options. Issues may range from delays, safety concerns, and overcharging in public transportation to problems with taxis, rideshare services, and airlines.

Explore

Cybercrime Complaint

Cybercrime complaints involve reports of illegal activities in the digital realm, such as hacking, online fraud, identity theft, and cyberbullying. Victims report these crimes to seek investigation and legal action against perpetrators, contributing to the fight against digital threats.

Explore

Child Welfare Complaint

Child welfare complaints pertain to concerns about the well-being, safety, or rights of children. They encompass issues like child abuse, neglect, exploitation, custody disputes, and violations of child protection laws, reported to relevant authorities for the protection and welfare of children.
